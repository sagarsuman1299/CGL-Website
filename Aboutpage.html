The journey of CGS began as Gamelab in 2018, focusing on the development of stylised games and the graphic requirements for the same. Since then we have flourished into a multi-faceted organisation driven to greater achievements, fueled by our ambition. Our topics of interest range from our original mission of Game Development to advanced application of Machine Learning, AR/VR, Procedural Graphics and much more.

At the Computer Graphics Society - IIT Kharagpur we work on the technology of the future. We are a group of students passionate in the development of immersive games, graphics and AR/VR technologies that we believe will form the cornerstone for the next paradigm shift.
Our primary tool for game development is the game engine, a place where we can easily integrate various features and produce renders. This includes the Unity Game Engine and Unreal Engine. A game is incomplete without it's assets, for this we use Blender, a brilliant open source, all-in-one software for creation of 3D and 2D assets. We also use Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator,After Effects and Da Vinci for Graphics. CGS also pursues numerous R&D projects, from OpenCV applications, Neural Networks to Embedded Electronics.
